Tour: Langtang Trek

The Langtang trekking tour is an awe-inspiring adventure that encapsulates the very essence of the Himalayas. At an elevation of 3856 meters, the Langtang Valley boasts panoramic views of the Himalayas, including Langtang Lirung (7,227m). This region is home to Langtang National Park, spanning over 1,710 square kilometers and hosting diverse wildlife, such as red pandas and Himalayan black bears. With over 70 glaciers, including the awe-inspiring Langtang Glacier, and over 400 species of birds, the Langtang trekking tour is a captivating journey that will leave you with memories to cherish for a lifetime.

Maximum elevation: 3856m
Estimated days of hiking: 5-8 Days
